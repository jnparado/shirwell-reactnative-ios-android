Shirwell Bancan (the “App”). Effective date: 25 April 2026.

This policy describes how the Shirwell Bancan mobile application handles information. The App is a browser-style wrapper that primarily displays content from https://shirwel.com/ and related pages you choose to open inside the App.

This policy applies to you when you install or use the App on your device.

3. Information the App may process

Unless we state otherwise in an updated version of this policy, we do not intend the App itself to collect a separate list of personal identifiers beyond what the embedded web experience and the platform normally involve.

4. How we use information

We use information as needed to operate and improve the App, to display the websites you choose, and to comply with law. We do not sell your personal information.

5. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us using the details below and we will take appropriate steps.

6. Security

We use reasonable measures to provide a secure experience, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Use the App and linked websites only on connections and devices you trust.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or the Shirwell Bancan App, use the contact or support options published on https://shirwel.com/. You can also use the developer contact details shown on this app’s Google Play store page.
